Our Destinations

Two Premium Locations

Discover luxury hospitality in Pakistan's most coveted destinations — the cosmopolitan capital and the serene mountain highlands.

Islamabad

Margalla Hills, Capital Territory

Our Islamabad properties are nestled in the foothills of the majestic Margalla Hills, offering panoramic views of lush green valleys and the city skyline. Located minutes from Faisal Mosque, Daman-e-Koh viewpoint, and the Centaurus Mall, these fully furnished service apartments provide the perfect blend of urban convenience and natural tranquility.

Ideal for business professionals, diplomatic delegates, medical tourists visiting PIMS or Shifa Hospital, and families seeking a luxurious staycation near Margalla Hills National Park hiking trails.

Margalla Views Fine Dining Airport Shuttle

Nathia Gali

Galyat, Abbottabad District

Escape to the misty pine forests of Nathia Gali, perched at 8,200 feet elevation in the Galyat mountain range. Our mountain retreats are surrounded by ancient deodar and pine forests, just minutes from Ayubia National Park, Mushkpuri Peak, and the historic Nathia Gali Church.

Perfect for honeymoon couples, adventure trekkers exploring the Pipeline Track and Miranjani Summit, and families looking for a cool mountain getaway during Islamabad's hot summers. Experience crisp mountain air and starlit skies.
